Magic Eight Ball

Shake it vigorously while whispering sweet nothings, then listen closely for the whispers of destiny. Concentrate on your burning question and flip the eight ball for its mystical message. Embrace the response with humor and gratitude, rinse and repeat for future insights, and blame any questionable life choices on the whimsical orb. Now, armed with your newfound wisdom, face life's challenges with confidence and a dash of fortune-telling flair!
